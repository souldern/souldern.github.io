Local Services

Post Office

The nearest Post Office is at Wrighton's in Fritwell. Postal services are also available at Souldern Village hall on Thursday Mornings. The village has three post boxes, on the Hill by the junction with Bovewell, at the end of Back Lane opposite the Pond, and on the High Street opposite the end of Bates Lane.

Library

The nearest library is Deddington Library.

Please consider supporting the local library, see http://www.friendsofdeddingtonlibrary.org/.

Police

The nearest police station is at Bicester. Our local police are part of the Bicester Rural Neighbourhood Policing Team who can be contacted on the Non Emergency Number 101.

Health Centre

The nearest GP Surgery is at Deddington health centre.

Bus

The village is on the 81/81A bus route operated by Heyfordian Travel. The current timetable is available to download.

There is also a free weekly bus service to Bicester. See the Announcement and 2018 timetable for details of this service which is funded by Oxfordshire county council.
