Notes

  • Enter keywords from house names, road names, or postcodes into the box above.
  • Each house is shown with an image (which is also a link to Google Street View).
  • Villagers are welcome to submit new (square) images for their houses, or any other details that they wish to list by the house entry.
  • If you use the Update URL button you can save a search and use it in an email etc. So that for example you can link to https://www.souldern.org/home/houses/?h=willow to list the houses with Willow in their name.
  • Initial images derived from Google Street view, in accordance with their terms of use, or photographs by David Carlisle for this site, or in some cases from freely available images.
