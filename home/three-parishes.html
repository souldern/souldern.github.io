The Three Parishes

Until December 2021, Ardley-with-Fewcott, Fritwell and Souldern had a magazine, The Three Parishes. It covered all aspects of the village communities and was delivered by volunteers in each of the villages.

The issues 2018–2021 are still available from the Three Parishes magazine site.

Three Parishes Ardley Fritwell Souldern Facebook Group is still active
