Steven Mead with Brackley & District Band: 22 September, Stowe School

posted 30 Aug 2018, 11:55 by David Carlisle

Brackley & District Band is proud to announce that the world-renowned euphonium player, Steven Mead, will be performing a masterclass with members of the band along with musicians from Stowe School at the school on Saturday 22 September.

This will culminate in a massed band concert at 7pm at the school, details as below:




Concert details
Date/time: 22 September 2018, 7pm
Running time: c. 2 hours including interval
Location: Ugland Auditorium, Stowe School

Tickets: £12 full price / £10 concessions
