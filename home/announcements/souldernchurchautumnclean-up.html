Souldern Church Autumn Clean-up

posted 2 Sep 2017, 02:06 by David Carlisle
Saturday 30th September

A seasonal  clean up of the church, inside and out, is planned from 10am. Volunteers with garden tools and/or cleaning equipment will be most welcome and will be fuelled with bacon butties and coffee throughout the day. 

Even if you’ve only got an hour to spare your help would be most appreciated please.
