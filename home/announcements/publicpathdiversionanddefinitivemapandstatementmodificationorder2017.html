Public Path Diversion and Definitive Map and Statement Modification Order 2017

posted 19 Jul 2017, 17:12 by David Carlisle   [ updated 19 Jul 2017, 17:14 ]
The Oxfordshire County Council
Souldern Footpath No 1 (part)
Public Path Diversion and Definitive Map and Statement Modification Order 2017
 
On 22nd June 2017, the Oxfordshire County Council made the above Order under section 119 of the Highways Act 1908 and Section 53A(2) of the Wildlife and Countryside Act 1981. The Order, if confirmed, will divert part of the public footpath as described and shown in the Order.
 
A Map and Notice publicising the Order has been placed on the village notice board, and is also available to view online at https://consultations.oxfordshire.gov.uk/consult.ti/souldernfp1madeordernotice/consultationHome.
 
Any representations about or objections to the Order may be sent in writing to the Senior Rights of Way Officer, Oxfordshire County Council, Definitive Map and Commons, Speedwell House, Speedwell street, Oxford OX1 1NE, not later than 18th August 2017
