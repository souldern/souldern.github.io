Parish Clerk Vacancy

posted 2 Nov 2016, 12:53 by David Carlisle

Souldern Parish Council

A vacancy exists for a new

Parish Clerk

An exciting opportunity to be involved in the shaping of our community

The flexible working hours are salaried and cover a wide variety of tasks in the local community including:

  • Ensuring that the legal and statutory provisions affecting the Council’s activities are observed
  • Managing all postal and electronic communications for the Council and liaising with public bodies and agencies (a laptop will be provided)
  • Preparing the agenda, taking and writing up minutes of meetings
  • Posting notices, agendas etc to website and notice boards in the Parish
