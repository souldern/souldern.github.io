# RAF Croughton Training Exercises

**Message from RAF Croughton**

We'd like to make you aware of some temporary changes you may notice
at RAF Croughton from today onwards. In light of current real-world
events, the base will be taking precautionary steps to ensure the
personnel and facilities remain prepared and secure.

As a result, you may observe an increased security presence at the
base, including personnel carrying weapons. You may also hear or see
training exercises taking place on site. The 'Giant Voice' tannoy
system will be used frequently to broadcast emergency messages as part
of these exercises. We want to reassure you that these are training
exercises and there is no immediate threat to the base or surrounding
community. These measures are part of drills designed to ensure
readiness and reflect the current global situation.

Please share this information within the local community as soon as
possible to help ensure awareness and to minimise any potential alarm
or confusion.

We appreciate your understanding and continued support. If you have
any concerns or questions, please do not hesitate to reach out. Please
be aware that we cannot answer questions about the global operation
taking place. Should you have such questions, please direct these to
the Public Affairs office who can be reached on
[501csw.pa@us.af.mil](mailto:501csw.pa@us.af.mil).
