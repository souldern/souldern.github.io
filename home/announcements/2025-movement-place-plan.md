## Summary

[The Movement and Place (MAP) Plan for Bicester and Mid Cherwell](https://letstalk.oxfordshire.gov.uk/45469/widgets/137139/documents/94539) is Oxfordshire County Council’s plan to improve local areas and transport. It aims to make public spaces better for the community and upgrade travel routes and infrastructure.


[This Plan](https://letstalk.oxfordshire.gov.uk/45469/widgets/137139/documents/94539) is a ‘live’ document, which means it will be updated regularly to keep up with changes and future growth. It includes ideas and projects for the next 25 years.


The intention is to support sustainable development, make it easier for people to get around, improve the environment, and help make life better for everyone who lives, works, or visits Bicester and Mid Cherwell.
