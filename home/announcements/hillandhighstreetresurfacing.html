Hill and High Street Resurfacing

posted 29 Sep 2017, 02:10 by David Carlisle   [ updated 1 Oct 2017, 10:16 ]
Hill and High Street Resurfacing (updated text)
 

The roadworks are now scheduled to commence on Thursday 5th October and work will take place between 9 am and 3.30 pm.

Work will be taking place for micro asphalt resurfacing of the Hill and High Street, probably during the first week of October, between the hours of 9.30 am and 3.30 pm. The exact dates are not yet confirmed due to issues with the weather and contractor availability. All affected households will receive an information leaflet before the work begins, and will be asked to remove their vehicles whilst the work takes place.

Access into the village will be maintained at all times, although there may be some short delays.

 

The Parish Council did not receive any prior notice about this, but have now requested to be kept updated and will post any further information here as it becomes available.
