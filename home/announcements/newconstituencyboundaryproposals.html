New constituency boundary proposals

posted 14 Sep 2016, 08:10 by David Carlisle




As you may have heard, new parliamentary constituency boundaries have been proposed, the full details are available at the Boundary Commission's portal site.
As shown in the above maps, the main change affecting our region is the loss of the villages to the South and East of Bicester which would form part of a new Henley and Thame constituency.
