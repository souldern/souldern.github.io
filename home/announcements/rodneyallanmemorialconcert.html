Rodney Allan Memorial Concert

posted 27 Sep 2015, 14:52 by Souldern Village   [ updated 27 Sep 2015, 14:52 ]
A concert in memory of Rodney Allan will be held at 7:00pm Saturday 24th October in St Mary's Church.

Souldern Church Choir will be performing a selection of Rodney’s favourites including Mozart and Gilbert & Sullivan.
