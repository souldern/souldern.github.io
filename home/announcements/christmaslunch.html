Christmas Lunch

posted 26 Nov 2016, 15:22 by David Carlisle   [ updated 28 Nov 2016, 00:03 ]

As announced in the recent newsletter, a Christmas lunch in the village hall is being arranged on December 7th.

Residents should by now have received invitations.

Following from the Souldern Vision survey, we have not done enough to involve our more senior members of the community. We are wishing to remedy this situation, and so a Christmas lunch is being organised that will take place on 7th December in the village hall and will be funded by some of the proceeds from the recent Autumn Sale organised by Linda Palmer.

The Village Networks Experts Roadshow will be attending the lunch and will be able to offer practical help and advice on matters that are of concern and there will also be ‘time to talk’ on suggestions for future get togethers and events so all suggestions welcome. Please look out for further details or contact Barbara (345931) if you would like to come.
