Souldern Church Harvest Weekend

posted 2 Sep 2017, 02:10 by David Carlisle
Harvest Weekend—7th & 8th October

Village Consultation:

The church will be open all weekend with displays and proposals for improving facilities, making it more accessible and more welcoming to  all who use it.

Whether you come to church for services, events or just spend some quiet time on your own this is your church and your opinions matter.

Should improvements include toilet facilities, a servery, better heating……?? 

This is your church and the PCC would really appreciate your comments and feedback so please come along and have your say .

Bring & Share Harvest Supper—Saturday 7th October 7pm in the village hall

Harvest Festival Service—Sunday 8th October 11am in the church
