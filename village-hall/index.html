The village hall is fully accessible and offers off street parking.

It is managed by a committee, elected annually, representing all the village associations.

The hall is by the playing field at the entrance to the village at the top of “The Hill”, as shown on the linked map.

The interior of the hall has been recently renovated with new flooring and new kitchen area fittings. The main hall has an 80 person capacity, and there is a smaller room which can be used independently as a meeting or committee room, or in conjunction with the main hall.

The Hall is adjacent to the new village playground with which it shares car parking.

The village hall has wifi and broadband internet provided by Gigaclear.
