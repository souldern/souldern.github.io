Sports Field

The Sports field is at the top of the Hill, adjacent to the Village Hall and the Playground. It is the regular home ground for Souldern Football team as well as being used for general recreation and Village events such as Games at the Souldern Solstice and Jubilee celebrations. It is also used to host a regular series of car boot sales in the summer which raise funds for a range of Souldern organisations.
