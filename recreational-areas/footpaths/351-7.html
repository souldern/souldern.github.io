351/7

Foxhill Lane to Crown Cottage

Footpath map


Presumably originally an access path to the Crown Public house, this path starts at a stone stile on Foxhill Lane.


Following the path between trees and the hedge along the garden of Pleasant View (formally Bicester Close).


And then a path between the old stone wall and wooden fencing of the houses to the left.


The track originally emerged at a gate adjacent to Crown Cottage on Bates Lane, but was recently redirected and now turns left for a short fenced section.


Ending by the footpath sign on the road "Hedges and Ditches".
