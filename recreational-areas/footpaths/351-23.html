351/23

Fox Lane to Foxhill Lane Bridleway

Footpath (erroneously marked as 351/8 on the definitive map pdf) map


Start from the stile over the gate off Foxhill Lane, near the junction with Fox Lane.


The fenced and tree lined path follows the edge of the gardens of the houses along Foxhill Lane.


Finally entering the field via a metal stile.


Follow the field boundary, climbing the hill alongside Foxhill Lane Bridleway,


To arrive at a stone stile leading to the Foxhill Lane bridleway.
