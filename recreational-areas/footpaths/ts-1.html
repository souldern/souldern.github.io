Tusmore Road

Tusmore Road is a public road but included here as it links many of the footpaths in the village.


The road starts from the junction opposite the Hill at the entrance to the village.


View looking away from Souldern.


Clear views over towards Croughton Airbase.


Passing Tower Farm.


Just past Tower Farm, pass the bridleway from the B4100 to Croughton. Turning right here gives an easy route back to Souldern via Fritwell, or turning left gives a route back via Croughton and Aynho or The footpath to Souldern via Upper Ayhno Grounds.


Signpost at the Bridelway.


Aproaching Roundhill Farm.


At this junction all traffic is now diverted to the left as the old road ahead is blocked by the A43. If walking to Tusmore, follow this guide which follows the road over the road bridge, however the old road is still open as footpath from Roundhill Farm to the A43.


Footpath signs: left is the old road to the A43, right is the footpath back to Souldern via footpath from B4100 to Tusmore.


The road towards Croughton.


Turn right at this junction to reach Tusmore via the road bridge, or keep straight on to reach Croughton.


The A43 from the road bridge.
