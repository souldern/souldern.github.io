351/18

Wharf Lane to Souldern Grounds Lane

Footpath map


Starting at a stile in the layby at the Manor entrance to Wharf Lane, this path crosses grass fields, initially following the line of the path to the Green.


Across a stile in the middle of the long fence separating the two fields. At the north edge of this fence, the path to the Green enters the wood to the left but this path crosses straight over the next field to the gate and stile into the wood along side the road.


Finally the path passes through a small wood and across a recently renovated footbridge to a stile onto Souldern Grounds Lane.


Looking back from stile Souldern Grounds Lane.
