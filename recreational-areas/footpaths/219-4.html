219/4

Somerton Road to Fritwell

Footpath map


From the Motorway Bridge on the road to Somerton, at the top of Foxhill Lane, this footpath crosses the fields to Fritwell.


Entering a tree-lined path via a stile.


Then through this gate into an open field,


to follow the field boundary.


A stiles and gates then lead into another path through the trees.


Clear path through the trees.


Chickens and bees on to the right.


Approaching Fritwell.


The path then crosses a small courtyard.


A short road between buildings leads from the courtyard.


Finally emerging on North Street in Fritwell, opposite the Church.

From here you could walk along the road through Fritwell and back to Souldern, or a short walk along the road towards the edge of the village leads to the path to Hill House.
