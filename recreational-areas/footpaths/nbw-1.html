Nancy Bowles Wood

Private Land (Public Access) map


Not a designated footpath, the wood has been created on land donated to Souldern village.

There are more pictures and information about the wood on its own page on this site.


The main footpath leaves Foxhill Lane Bridleway at the sign.


The path drops down through the wood.


At the end of the wood, a marked panoramic view back over the fields towards Souldern.
