Towpath past Somerton

This page describes the section of the Oxford Canal towpath, immediately to the south of the section described on the Canal Towpath page.


The section starts at Somerton bridge (bridge 196), which may be reached from Souldern by the Canal Towpath, Upper Souldern Grounds to Somerton or Somerton Road.

Approaching the Somerton–Heyford Road.


The end of the track on Somerton–Heyford road. From here you can turn left towards Somerton and return to Souldern via the routes listed at the start, or turn right towards Heyford and then return to Souldern via the path from Somerton Road to Heyford.
