351/21

The Tchure

Footpath map


The Tchure (a local word for alley) forms a useful shortcut across the village from Foxhill Lane


Initially a drive as far as Tchure House.


The footpath is then a narrow alley alongside the building.


Initially between the side of the house and a stone wall.


Then between a hedge and low wall.


Approaching Bates Lane.


The stile onto Bates Lane.
