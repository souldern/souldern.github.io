351/8

Foxhill Lane to Fritwell Road

Bridleway map


Leaving the Foxhill Lane Bridleway at a junction just before Nancy Bowles Wood,


The path follows a field boundary, Initially hedged on both sides.


The field hedge gives way to a fence. .


Connecting with the road at the junction between the Fritwell and Somerton roads


The sign pointing back to Souldern.

A short walk along the road will lead to the top of Foxhill Lane, to make a circular walk back to Souldern.
