351/20

Wharf Lane to Parish Boundary

Footpath map


The path starts from Wharf Lane between the railways and the motorway.

The previously hard to find stile has recently been opened out to a wide farm entrance, with a footpath marker.


It then follows the field boundary heading north towards Ockley Brook (this is the view looking back towards Wharf lane).


Finally crossing Ockley Brook to enter Northamptonshire.


It joins the path from Souldern Mill to Aynho Wharf at the field edge on the other side of the stream.
