351/12b

Upper Souldern Grounds to Somerton Road

Bridleway map


Originally this bridleway was a route from the village to Somerton road, but that route is now blocked by the M40, so it is more natural to start from the Somerton road end, which can be reached by a short walk along the road from the top of Foxhill Lane.

The start of the bridleway leaving Somerton Road.


The good track goes down the hill towards Upper Souldern grounds


ending at the trees to the left of the farm.


Where a small bridge and a stile mark the start of the footpath to Somerton.
