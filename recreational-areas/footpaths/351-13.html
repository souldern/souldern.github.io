351/13

Foxhill Lane

Bridleway map


The bridleway starts at the corner at the end of Foxhill Lane road, and goes south uphill to meet the Somerton Road.


The initial section after passing the houses.


On the left the bridleway passes the stile leading to the footpath back to the village.


Further up the hill is the junction with the bridleway to the Fritwell road, bear to the right to stay on this main bridleway.


The entrance to Nancy Bowles Wood.


After the Wood the path is less used and can be more overgrown, it dips into a hollow rising past Foxhill Barn.


Then as a good track up to a gate on the Somerton Road.
