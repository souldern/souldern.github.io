Sunday 8 May Kids on Sunday

posted 15 May 2016, 02:37 by Souldern Village

We spent a very happy hour in the chapel schoolroom thinking about the importance of building firm foundations. We experimented with rocks and sand and reminded ourselves of the parable about the wise man who built his house upon a rock. We took away the text from psalm 18 - "The Lord is my rock." Coffee, squash and rocky road were enjoyed.



Please feel free to join us on 15 May at the chapel for youth worship 2.30pm and on 22 May at St Mary's for the next Kids on Sunday (Pentecost). All ages welcome.
