WILLIAM FREDERICK ROBBINS

Corporal, 7th Battalion, The Oxfordshire and Buckinghamshire Light Infantry, 78 Brigade, 26th Division. Army no. 21257

He was killed in action on 8 May 1917 during the Battle of Doiran. He was 29 He was the son of Joseph and Mary Robbins of Oxford and the husband of Ethel Alice Robbins, 3 Tichborne Row, Paddington. He is remembered on the Doiran Memorial, Macedonia.

Additional Information

Birth Place: Oxford
Residence: London
Enlistment Location: Oxford
