Arthur John LAKE William Frederick ROBBINS Memorial 1914–18

JOHN HENRY PAYNE

Private, 1st Battalion, The Oxfordshire and Buckinghamshire Light Infantry, 17 Brigade, 6th (Poona) Division. Army no. 9076

He was killed in action on 22 November 1915. He was 25 He was the son of the late James and of Sarah Payne of Souldern He is remembered on panel 26 and 63 of the Basra Memorial

Additional Information

Birth Place: Drayton
Enlistment Location: Banbury
He was a Regular soldier.
