WILLIAM JOHN SWEETMAN

Private, 2nd Battalion, The Oxfordshire and Buckinghamshire Light Infantry, 5 Brigade, 2nd Division. Army no. 18465

He was killed in action on 30 July 1916 in the fighting on the Somme. He was 18 He was the grandson of Elizabeth Swetman, The Green, Souldern He is remembered on pier and face 10A and 10D of the Thiepval Memorial.

Additional Information

Residence: Souldren
Enlistment Location: Oxford
