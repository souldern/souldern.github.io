WILLIAM JAMES CALLOW

Private, 2nd/4th Battalion, The Oxfordshire and Buckinghamshire Light Infantry, 184 Brigade, 61st Division. Army no. 240310

He was killed in action on 22 August 1917 in the fighting around Ypres. He was 27 He was the husband of Florence Phoebe Callow, Chapel Row, Souldern. He is remembered on panel 96 to 98 of the Tyne Cot Memorial.

Additional Information

Residence: Souldern
Enlistment Location: Oxford
