WILLIAM ROBERT ROBBINS

Private, 1st/4th Battalion, The Oxfordshire and Buckinghamshire Light Infantry, 145 Brigade, 48th Division. Army no. 203330. He was formerly with the Queen’s Own Oxfordshire Hussars, Army no. 2561

He was killed in action on 15 June 1918 in the Battle of the Piave, fighting on the Asiago Plateau. He was 19 He was the son of Frederick and Fanny Robbins of Souldern He is buried in plot 3 row A grave 14 Boscon British Cemetery, Italy.

Additional Information

Birth Place: Souldern
Residence: Souldern
Enlistment Location: Oxford
