WALTER THOMAS WESTBURY

Private, 2nd Battalion, The Oxfordshire and Buckinghamshire Light Infantry, 5 Brigade, 2nd Division. Army no. 18149

He was killed in action on 31 July 1916 in the fighting around Delville Wood. He was 18 He was the son of Frederick and Sarah Westbury, The Green, Souldern He is buried in plot 2 row A grave 1 Corbie Communal Cemetery Extension.

Additional Information

Birth Place: Souldern
Residence: Souldern
Enlistment Location: Oxford
