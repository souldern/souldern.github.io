ARTHUR JOHN LAKE

Private, 9th Battalion, The Worcestershire Regiment, 39 Brigade, 13th Division. Army no. 27547

He was killed in action on 25 January 1917 attacking the Hai Salient during the Battle of Kut al Imara. He was 19 He was the son of Stephen and Esther E Lake He is buried in grave XVIII C 3 Amara War Cemetery

Additional Information

Birth Place: Souldern
Residence: Souldern
Enlistment Location: Birmingham
