FREDERICK HENRY SWEETMAN

Private, 2nd Battalion, The Oxfordshire and Buckinghamshire Light Infantry, 5 Brigade, 2nd Division. Army no. 25850

He died of his wounds on 30 April 1917 probably received at the Battle of Arleux. He was 36 He was the son of the late George and of Elizabeth Sweetman and the husband of Sarah Sweetman of Souldern He is buried in grave II J 34 Aubigny Communal Cemetery Extension.

Additional Information

Birth Place: Solgrave
Residence: Souldern
Enlistment Location: Banbury
The town of Aubigny was a hospital centre
