Kenneth Sutherland RUDD

William Robert ROBBINS Frederick Henry SWEETMAN Memorial 1914–18

KENNETH SUTHERLAND RUDD

Captain, 10th Battalion, The Prince of Wales’s Own (West Yorkshire Regiment), 50 Brigade, 17th Division.

He was killed in action on 10 October 1918 during the Allied pursuit to the Selle. He was 24 He was the son of the late Rev Prebendary Eric J S Rudd of Souldern and of Edyth T H Rudd, Whitehall, Goodrich, Ross, Herefordshire He is buried in grave II F 17 Caudry British Cemetery.

Additional Information

The plaque states that he is buried at Audencourt, near Cambrai. Subsequently those interred in Audencourt have been moved to Caudry.
