WILLIAM HENRY BATES

Corporal, C Company, 3rd Battalion, The Worcestershire Regiment, 57 Brigade, 19th Division. Army no. 207283

He was killed in action on 27 May 1918 in what became the final push of the war. He was 23 He was the son of Joseph and Emma Mary Bates, West End, Souldern He is buried in grave I D 1 Marfaux British Cemetery near Reims.

Additional Information

Birth Place: Souldern
Residence: Souldern
Enlistment Location: Oxford
