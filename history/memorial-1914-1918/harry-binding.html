William Henry BATES Sidney BLENCOWE Memorial 1914–18

HARRY BINDING

Private, 2nd/5th Battalion, The Manchester Regiment, 199 Brigade, 66th Division. Army no. 202605

He was killed in action on 8 October 1917 in the third Battle of Ypres. He was 26 He was the son of John Binding, 671 Sixth Street, Trafford Park, Manchester. He is remembered on panel 120-124 and 162-162A and 163A of the Tyne Cot Memorial

Additional Information

Birth Place: Buxton, Derby
Residence: Trafford Park, Manchester
Enlistment Location: Ashton-under-lyne
