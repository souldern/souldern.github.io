The original PDF is available at the Bodleian site, this was processed for this site with Adobe Acrobat to produce a PDF with text search (11.4MB)  generated from an OCR scan of the document.

The same OCR text was then exported and checked and rekeyed as needed to correct for errors and reformatted as HTML for this website. The formatting uses a serif style and similar text width to the original, but does not attempt to preserve line and page breaks. Some care has been taken to check the transcription against the PDF scan, but some errors probably remain and may be reported via the web form on this site.

Much of the text of this book was taken from the Notes on Souldern by J. H. and A. P. Gough, 1882, A PDF version of which is available from this site.
