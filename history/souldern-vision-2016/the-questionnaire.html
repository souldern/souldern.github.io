THE QUESTIONNAIRE

‘A Vision for Souldern’ was launched on the 20th June 2015. Residents were asked to give their many opinions and ideas on village life here in Souldern using post-it notes. The contents of these post-it notes were then used to help to compile a resident’s questionnaire to gauge the depth of feeling across a broad range of topics within our community. The questionnaires were then circulated to every household in the Parish of Souldern.

66% of households responded to the survey. This represents 268 residents from 118 households. These figures should be borne in mind when reading the following analysis, however there is no reason to suppose they do not reflect a cross section of opinions in the village.

We would like to thank everyone who contributed by completing the questionnaire.
