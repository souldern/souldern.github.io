SOULDERN
SELF PORTRAIT OF A VILLAGE

In 1989 The Parish council initiated a survey of the village residents.

The purpose of the Appraisal was to gather information on a variety of topics relevant to Souldern, which could be used as a guide when considering what changes should be supported or opposed.

The booklet drawn up analysing the results of the survey is presented in these pages.
